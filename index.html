Part 1: Topic and Tasks

This project's topic is diamonds, namely using data and visualizations to find what the strongest indicator of price for diamonds is using key attributes.

Data Introduction:

The dataset used in this analysis contains 53,940 records of diamonds, with each diamond described by 11 attributes related to its physical and qualitative characteristics. The dataset provides valuable insights into factors that influence the pricing of diamonds, including their carat weight, cut, color, and clarityâ€”key determinants in the diamond industry.

  • Price: US Dollars
  • Carat: Weight of the diamond
  • Cut: Quality of the cut (Fair, Good, Very Good, Premium, Ideal)
  • Color: Color Quality (J (worst) to D (best))
  • Clarity: How clear the diamond is (I1 (worst), SI2, SI1, VS2, VS1, VVS2, VVS1, IF (best))
  • x: length in millimeters
  • y: width in millimeters
  • z: depth in millimeters
